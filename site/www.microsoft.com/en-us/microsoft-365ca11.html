Frequently asked questions 

  • The Microsoft Copilot app is a conversational chat experience. Microsoft 365 Copilot for individuals brings Copilot to apps such as Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Outlook. 
  • There are several types of functionalities today including the ability to reference a file to begin a document or presentation in Word and PowerPoint respectively, as well as the ability to generate an image with Designer directly from your Word or PowerPoint canvas from the Copilot chat panels. We are continuing to evolve how Copilot acts across your Microsoft 365 apps as consumers begin to leverage Copilot.
  • Copilot brings the power of AI to the Microsoft 365 apps you use every day to boost your creativity, realize your potential, and communicate with confidence. With Copilot, you can effortlessly plan your next trip or event with OneNote, polish up your resume and cover letter in Word, quickly write great emails with the right tone, and catch up on long email threads in Outlook, or quickly produce slides for your next PowerPoint party. Copilot is your AI companion to work alongside you, taking care of the busy work so you can stay in the flow and make time for things that spark joy. 
  • Microsoft 365 Family and Microsoft 365 Premium are designed for individual use and can be shared with up to six people—excluding AI features, which are only available to the subscription owner. For workplace collaboration, we recommend Microsoft 365 business plans like Microsoft 365 Copilot and Microsoft 365 Business Basic, Standard, or Premium, which are built specifically for teams and organizations. See Copilot plans for every organization.
  • Access to AI features in Microsoft 365 apps like Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook and higher usage of AI features in apps like Designer are only available to the subscription owner of a Microsoft 365 Family plan and cannot be shared with others.
  • The Frontier program offers early access to experimental Microsoft 365 Copilot features—designed for customers and creators to explore cutting-edge AI innovations before general availability. These features are still in development and may change over time. Learn more about our Microsoft Frontier program.
  • With Copilot, you can conveniently tap into the power of AI in the Microsoft 365 apps that you use every day, without the need to toggle between screens or tabs, making it a more seamless experience. Copilot can even apply formatting to your content in the Microsoft 365 apps, taking advantage of hundreds of features many people rarely use. And users can share and collaborate in real-time on content generated by Copilot in apps like Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
