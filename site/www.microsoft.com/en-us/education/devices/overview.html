Affordable learning devices

Use Windows 11 educational devices online or offline, protect against online threats, and get built-in features that help students achieve their potential.
  • Get affordable Windows educational devices that support students and educators where learning takes place, without relying on a network connection.
  • Provide students with inclusive, accessible Microsoft software and tools. Support the social and emotional wellbeing of all learners with built-in assistive technology at no extra cost. 
  • Help protect learning devices with built-in antivirus and minimize online classroom disruptions with integrated security features.
  • Use Windows 11's built-in AI to streamline administrative tasks and IT operations, enhancing classroom management. The new Copilot+ PC features on-device processing for improved security, efficiency, privacy, accessibility, and offline functionality.
