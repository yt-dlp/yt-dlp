Frequently asked questions

  • Both Microsoft 365 Education and Office 365 Education have various pricing options for schools. View the product comparison table and contact a sales partner to discuss your school’s needs.
  • Microsoft donates Office 365 Education for the classroom to eligible institutions at no cost to ensure all students have access to tools for learning and building skills for the future. Office 365 Education provides web versions of Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote, Microsoft Teams, and Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat, plus additional AI-based tools for work and learning. Enter your school email address to get started. If you’re a school seeking additional assistance in signing up your school for Microsoft Office 365, Contact a sales partner.
  • Students and educators are eligible for a 10% discount on select items in the Microsoft Store with an active Microsoft account. (A Microsoft account is an email address and password that you use with Outlook.com (Hotmail), Office, OneDrive, Skype, Xbox, and/or Windows.) If you don’t have an active Microsoft account, see the Microsoft Store for discount details.
