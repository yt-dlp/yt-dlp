Frequently asked questions

  • Microsoft believes that when you create technologies that can change the world, you must also ensure that the technology is trustworthy. We are committed to creating Trustworthy AI by design. Our work is guided by a core set of principles: fairness, reliability and safety, privacy and security, inclusiveness, transparency, and accountability. We are putting those principles into practice across the company to develop and deploy AI that will have a positive impact on society. We take a cross-company approach through cutting-edge research, best-of-breed engineering systems, and excellence in policy and governance. Learn more about Trustworthy AI here.
  • To learn more about the tools available to you and how to plan your AI journey with Microsoft, please visit the Microsoft Education AI Toolkit.
  • Explore our insights on the opportunities and challenges in the Microsoft Special Report: AI in Education and Education Insights from the 2024 Work Trend Index Annual Survey.
  • We recommend getting started with resources such as our AI for Educators learning path and training resources, the AI Classroom Toolkit, Minecraft Education AI resources, and explore more content in our hub.
  • Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat with enterprise data protection is available at no additional cost for all educators, staff, and students aged 13+, GitHub Copilot is free for verified educators and students 13+, and all Microsoft 365 Education SKUs (including our free version) offer our AI-powered Learning Accelerators and new AI features designed to support educators in Teams for Education at no additional cost. Additionally, Khanmigo for Teachers is available at no cost to educators in over 40 countries and Minecraft Education offers free AI literacy resources.
  • Learn more about Copilot Chat with data protection, documentation including privacy and protections, our trustworthy AI approach, and use our Copilot Chat Starter Kit for Education.
  • Microsoft 365 Copilot is currently available for education organizations to purchase as an add-on for their educators, staff, and students aged 13+ through resellers or the Cloud Solution Provider (CSP) program. Please visit the Microsoft 365 Copilot page, or for more information, Microsoft 365 Copilot documentation.
  • Learn more about how to build your own copilots with Microsoft Foundry and Microsoft Copilot Studio.
