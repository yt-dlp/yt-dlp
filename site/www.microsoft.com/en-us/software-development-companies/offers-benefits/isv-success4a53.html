Eligibility

Are you eligible for benefits?

To participate in ISV Success partner companies must:

  1. Have an application or agent in development.
  2. Give the app or agent a business-to-business (B2B) focus.
  3. Intend to publish the app or agent to Marketplace.
  4. Join Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program.

  • ISV Success provides resources for building AI-powered apps with Microsoft Foundry, access to Azure Cognitive Services, and training sessions for using AI tools. Copilot developers can also take advantage of additional software company support, including events, expert consultations, and product benefits.
    Learn more

  • Software development companies who are building business-to-business (B2B) applications on the Microsoft Cloud and intend to publish through Microsoft Marketplace are eligible for ISV Success benefits—free for 12 months with no commitment to renew. 

     

    After 12 months, participants are eligible for a discounted renewal package with a $1,550 (USD) annual fee.

    Apply now
  • Yes, ISV Success benefits can complement other Microsoft offerings. You can’t stack Azure sponsorships, but you can toggle between subscriptions.
    Learn more

  • For renewal, you need to continue developing B2B apps, meet publication requirements, and maintain your Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program status. The annual renewal fee for the Core Package is $1,550 (USD). 

     

    For the Expanded Package, you’ll be invited by your ISV Success Engagement Manager based on performance criteria and intent to transact through Marketplace.

    Learn more
  • Through Marketplace Rewards, software development companies receive sales and marketing support, including listing optimization, social promotion, and Azure Sponsorship to create and run demos—helping you significantly boost marketplace sales.
    Learn more

  • ISV Success helps facilitate the publication of transactable offers through Microsoft Marketplace, which is essential for accessing co-sell benefits, including technical consultations that can help boost your listing and accelerate sales. 

     

    Additionally, you can access on-demand resources, Office Hours, and the Marketplace Community to get questions answered about transactable publishing.

    Learn more

  • ISV Success supports software development companies that offer transactable professional services only if the service is attached to a transactable app owned by the same software company. Publishing a professional service alone does not qualify for enrollment or renewal.

     

    For Marketplace Rewards, Marketplace Billed Sales (MBS) from professional services will not count towards the MBS tier. Only MBS from transactable apps will unlock Marketplace Rewards benefits.
