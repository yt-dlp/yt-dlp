    Frequently Asked Questions

    |

    • With a Microsoft gift card, you can buy apps, games, movies, and more from the Microsoft Store, on Windows PCs, and on Xbox. You can also use it to purchase subscriptions like Xbox Game Pass.

    • With an Xbox gift card, you can buy games, add-ons, in-game content, apps, movies, and TV shows on Xbox consoles and the Microsoft Store. You can also use it toward subscriptions like Xbox Game Pass.

    • You can buy Microsoft and Xbox gift cards online from the Microsoft Store or at participating retailers. Digital gift cards can be delivered instantly by email, making them a convenient option for gifting.

    • You can gift Xbox Game Pass by selecting “Buy as gift” when purchasing a Game Pass subscription, and you’ll be able to send it directly to the recipient.

      You can also give a Microsoft or Xbox gift card, which the recipient can use to purchase Game Pass themselves.

    • Xbox and Microsoft gift cards can be used on the Microsoft Store online, on Xbox consoles, and on Windows PCs. You can use the balance to buy Xbox games, apps, movies, devices, and subscriptions like Xbox Game Pass.

    • You can send a digital Xbox gift card or Microsoft gift card by selecting it online and choosing the option to send it by email. The recipient will receive a code they can redeem on their account.
