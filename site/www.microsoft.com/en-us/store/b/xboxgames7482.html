Frequently asked questions

|

  • To play a physical disc on Xbox Series X, insert the disc or search your library and select the game you want to play. However, the Xbox Series S is an all-digital gaming console for disc-free gaming. Disc-based games are not compatible with the Xbox Series S.

  • Xbox Series X is backwards compatible with thousands of games across four generations of Xbox. With Smart Delivery games, you can buy a game once and get the best version of that game for the console you’re playing on. To play a backwards compatible game, insert the disc or search your library and select the game you want to play.

  • Xbox Series S is also backwards compatible with thousands of games across four generations of Xbox. With Smart Delivery games, you can buy a digital game once and get the best version of that game for the console you’re playing on. Because Xbox Series S is all-digital, to play a backwards compatible game just access your digital game library through the console and select the game you want to play.

  • All Xbox Game Studio titles include Smart Delivery, so you get the best version of the game no matter which console you play on. Partner games that have chosen to include Smart Delivery will make note of the feature on their digital game page or on the box the disc comes in.

  • Your games need to be downloaded to your device whether they’re physical or digital, and they take up the same amount of space. However, digital games give you more flexibility, like remote installation and pre-loading before games launch. Learn more about buying digital games on Xbox and remotely installing and managing games here.

    More and more games are also using FastStart technology that allows you to start the game twice as fast while the rest of the game finishes downloading. Get more specific details from Support.
