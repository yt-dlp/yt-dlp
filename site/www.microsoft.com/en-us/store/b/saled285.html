    Where can I find a Microsoft Store or Microsoft Experience Center near me?

    Does Microsoft have Black Friday sales?

    Yes, Microsoft has Black Friday sales. Microsoft Store offers discounts on a variety of products which may include laptops, tablets, software, accessories, and Xbox products during Black Friday. Additionally, we provide benefits like free shipping, price protection, and trade-in options.

    The Microsoft Black Friday sale for 2025 runs from November 20th to November 29th. During this period, you may see discounts on a variety of products, including Surface devices, Xbox consoles, games, and accessories. Be sure to check the Microsoft Store for specific deals and offers available throughout the sale period.

    Does Microsoft have a Rewards Program?

    Yes. Earning rewards is easy, simple, and fun. Just search, shop, or play with Microsoft and you’ll be on your way to earning more than ever. Learn more here.
    Does Microsoft offer discounts on any of its products to employees at eligible companies?

    What does Microsoft sell?
