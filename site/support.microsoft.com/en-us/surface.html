    Surface for Business support

    Find IT resources to plan, deploy, and manage Microsoft Surface for Business in commercial and enterprise environments. Access the Surface IT Toolkit to configure, secure, and manage Surface devices efficiently. Use the Surface self-serve portals to centralize Surface device management with unified warranty oversight, simplified single or bulk claims, health monitoring with alerts, and one interface for all support tasks.

    Copilot+ PC

    Explore the possibilities of how to transform the way you work and play through AI experiences. Copilot+ PCs and Windows 11 PCs from Surface help simplify daily tasks and get things done. The power to multitask, jumpstart your creative process, and have an AI assistant is at your fingertips.

    Support for Windows 10 ended on October 14, 2025

    Microsoft is no longer providing free software updates from Windows Update, technical assistance, or security fixes for Windows 10. Your PC will still work, but we recommend moving to Windows 11. Windows 11 offers a modern and efficient experience designed to meet current demands for heightened security.
